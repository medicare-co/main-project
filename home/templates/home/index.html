Your medicines,
one click away



Upload your prescriptions here, and order them whenever you want to, from your nearest Medical Store.
Yep. You read that right.


Save all your medical information together for the best experience. One click to your regular meds ordered.
As simple as that.


Once you get your meds, save them in our app. You can set daily reminders for these, as prescribed by your doctor.
Pretty helpful.



Completely Hassle-free

 

Let your doctor make the list on our app. A generated link can be shared to you, so you can then confirm your orders to proceed till delivery.
As easy as that.

Versatile & Easy to Use

 

Worried about forgetting to take your meds on time? We've gotch'ya. Use the in-built Reminders to set them as per your prescriptions.
If you've got a change in your dosage, no need to modify all the reminders. Just change the dosage in the settings, all the remaining reminders will be automatically changed.
Magical.
